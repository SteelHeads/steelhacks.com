FEB 10TH & 11TH, 2017 - PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

SteelHacks is an event hosted at the University of Pittsburgh, through the Department of Computer Science, for students at colleges & universities across the US who are passionate about getting their hands dirty using technology andi design.

This year is the third annual Steelhacks hackathon. Each year students from around the Pittsburgh area and beyond come together to hack and make cool stuff.

Registration for SteelHacks 2017 is coming soon!
